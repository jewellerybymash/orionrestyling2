Frequently Asked Questions About Paint Protection Film

1. What is paint protection film?

Paint protection film (PPF) is a transparent, durable material applied to a vehicles surface to protect it from scratches, chips, and environmental damage. At Orion Restyling, we use top-quality PPF to keep your vehicle looking pristine.

2. How long does paint protection film last?

With proper care and maintenance, paint protection film can last anywhere from 5 to 10 years. The longevity depends on various factors, including environmental conditions and how well the vehicle is maintained.

3. Can paint protection film be removed?

Yes, paint protection film can be safely removed without damaging the underlying paint. Our expert team at Orion Restyling is equipped to handle the removal process, ensuring your vehicle is protected during the transition.

4. Does paint protection film change the appearance of my vehicle?

When applied correctly, paint protection film is nearly invisible and will not alter your vehicles appearance. It provides a glossy finish that enhances the paint while protecting it from damage.

5. Is paint protection film worth the investment?

Yes, investing in paint protection film is worthwhile if you want to preserve the beauty of your vehicle and reduce the risk of damage from daily wear and tear. It offers long-term protection, ultimately saving you money on repairs.

6. Can I wash my vehicle after installing paint protection film?

Yes, you can wash your vehicle after installing paint protection film. However, we recommend waiting at least 48 hours after installation for the adhesive to cure properly. We provide guidance on the best washing techniques for PPF maintenance.

7. Will PPF protect against swirl marks?

Paint protection film offers a level of protection against swirl marks and light scratches. However, it is still essential to follow proper washing techniques to minimize the risk of swirl marks forming on the film's surface.

8. How do I maintain my paint protection film?

To maintain your paint protection film, we recommend regular washing with a pH-neutral soap and avoiding abrasive materials. Our team at Orion Restyling will provide specific maintenance guidelines tailored to your vehicles needs.
